## DRIVER LICENCE REQUIREMENTS

### Accepted Licences
SIXT recognises all current **non-provisional, non-probationary** Australian licences and current overseas licences. Must be:
- In English with photo
- Produced at commencement of rental
- Not expired

If in another language or no photo → accept certified English translation or IDP. If no photo → IDP must accompany it.

### Licence Class Requirements
| Vehicle Type | Licence Required |
|---|---|
| Standard vehicle (up to 12 passengers) | Car (C) Licence |
| More than 12 people | **LR Licence** |
| Larger than 3 tonne / more than 21 people | **MR Licence** (6 tonne) |
| 8 tonne or larger | **HR Licence** |

### Age Requirements
| Age | Rules |
|---|---|
| Under 21 | **Cannot rent** |
| 21–24 (inclusive) | Age surcharge per day; standard range only; UP **not available** |
| 25+ | Full access; all protection products available |

### What to Bring at Pick-Up
- Booking confirmation number
- Driver's licence (physical copy)
- Physical credit or debit card (digital wallets NOT accepted for deposit)

### Zero Blood Alcohol Requirements (VIC)
Must have zero BAC at ALL times:
- Learner permit holders
- P1 or P2 licence holders
- Licence holders under 26
- Holders with E, I or Z condition
- Holders driving a bus, heavy vehicle, light vehicle towing heavy trailer, or commercial passenger vehicle

### Digital Driver's Licence — Verification (VIC, Service Victoria App)
1. Check **'Last Refreshed'** timestamp (pull down) — confirms live, not screenshot
2. Verify **photo** matches person
3. Check **Victorian Coat of Arms hologram** over photo
4. Check **QR code refreshes** every 2 minutes — static/non-refreshing = **RED FLAG**
5. Check **driver licence number** (tap + button)
6. Check **expiry date**
7. Check **date of birth**
8. Check **home address**
9. Check **customer's signature** — compare to rental agreement signature
10. Check **Licence Status** = green tick 'Current'; Proficiency = 'Full'

**Do not rent if licence status is not 'Current'.**

## UNDERSTANDING SIXT PAYMENTS

### Preauthorisation
At rental open, SIXT takes a **preauthorisation** of expected rental charge plus deposit/bond for extras (refuelling, extra time, mileage). Funds do NOT leave the account — frozen as pending transaction.

- System can only **GROW** a preauthorisation on an open rental — **CANNOT undo** an incremental authorisation done in error
- On vehicle return: final charges captured, remainder released

### Authorisation vs Capture
| Step | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **Authorisation** | Merchant requests approval; bank verifies and places hold; sends approved/declined response |
| **Capture** | Completes transaction by settling funds; occurs after authorisation |

### Pending Transactions — Key Points
- NOT yet reflected in current Account Balance
- Deducted from **Spendable Balance** (not Account Balance)
- Can take **2–5+ days** to settle
- Customer has committed funds — cannot be spent elsewhere
- Reduces existing available credit

## DAMAGE RECOVERY FEES (DRF)

| Protection Level | Passenger 2–6 Seats | 4WD/Ute/Premium/7–12 Seats | Vans/Trucks/Buses 13+ Seats |
|---|---|---|---|
| No Protection | Up to **$6,050** | Up to **$7,150** | Up to **$8,250** |
| Premium Protection (PP) | Up to **$1,100** | Up to **$2,200** | Up to **$2,200** |
| Ultimate Protection (UP) | **NIL** | **NIL** | **NIL** |

### Single Vehicle Accident Fee (SVAF)
Unless UP purchased, **SVAF of $2,200** applies IN ADDITION to DRF for single vehicle accidents when renting from **non-metro locations** and **ALL locations for commercial vehicles**.

### Damage Coverage
**NEVER covered (full cost payable regardless of protection):**
- Underbody damage
- Overhead damage
- Pantech damage
- Off-road damage
- Water damage

**Only covered with Ultimate Protection:**
- Windscreen damage
- Wheel and tyre damage
- Hail damage
- Lightning damage

## THE RENTAL CONTRACT (RentalTruth)

Four colour-coded sections:
- **Driver Details (pink)** — Hirer name, address, licence details, email
- **Vehicle Details (blue)** — Registration, make/model, vehicle class, rego expiry, fuel type
- **Rental Agreement (green)** — Agreement number, check out/in dates, KM out/in, fuel out/in, rental and return locations
- **Charges (red)** — Rental days (T), protections (BF/BC), vehicle reg fee (VL), CC fee (N2), GST, estimated total

### What the Customer Signs
- **Rental Agreement** — agrees to T&Cs and Linkt toll terms
- **Tolling** — charged by Tollaust/Linkt; $3.30 (inc. GST) service fee per day a toll is incurred
- **Fines** — customer responsible for all traffic/road fines + $66 (inc. GST) Fines Handling Fee per fine
- **Privacy** — SIXT collects personal information per Privacy Policy
- **Telematics** — tracking device may be fitted; location may be tracked
- **Payment Authority** — authorises SIXT/Linkt to charge card
- **Car Seat** — customer must ensure correctly fitted before leaving
- **Snow Cover** — if driving above snow line, must have purchased Snow Pass
- **Vehicle Condition** — customer confirms will check for damage and report before leaving

1. Log into Cobra → Reservation Center
2. Press Reservation → Reservation System
3. Complete location data: Location: Geelong | Date | Time | Days
4. Under Offer → press Car Group → magnifying glass
5. Select the vehicle wanted

### Display Arena Availability

3. Go to Vehicle History
4. Type in KMs and main tank (from Bluetooth device — do not guess)
5. Press Save Agreement
6. Press Close Check In

## BOOKING CONFIRMATION GUIDE (Geelong Branch)

Every upcoming reservation must be confirmed before pick-up date via: **phone call, voicemail (VML), or email**.

### Always Communicate
- ✓ Confirm booking date and time
- ✓ Remind: bring **physical** driver's licence
- ✓ Remind: bring **physical** credit or debit card
- ✗ Digital wallet payments (Apple Pay/Google Pay) NOT accepted for deposit

### Phone Call Script

**Opening:**
> "Hi, is this [customer name]? Great — this is [your name] calling from SIXT Geelong. I'm just calling to confirm your upcoming car rental booking."

**Confirm the details:**
> "I have you booked for [date] at [time], picking up from our Geelong branch — does that all still look correct for you?"

**Reminders:**
> "Perfect! Just a couple of things to have ready when you arrive — you'll need to bring your **physical driver's licence** (we can't accept digital licences for check-in), and a **physical credit or debit card** in your name for the deposit. Unfortunately we can't accept Apple Pay or Google Pay for the bond."

**If they ask about the deposit:**
> "The deposit is a preauthorisation — it's placed as a hold on your card but the funds aren't actually taken. It covers the rental cost plus a bond for any extras, and whatever isn't used is released when you return the vehicle. It can take a few days to clear on your end depending on your bank."

**If they ask about protection:**
> "We do offer a range of protection packages that can reduce or remove your damage liability. I can go through those options with you when you arrive, or if you'd like a quick overview now I'm happy to help."

**If they want to make a change (time, date, vehicle):**
> "Of course, I can look into that for you. [Make the change in Cobra / advise what's available.] I've updated that for you — you're now booked for [new detail]. Is there anything else I can help with?"

**If they want to cancel:**
> "No problem at all. I'll process that cancellation for you now. Can I just confirm your reservation number? [Process in Cobra.] That's all cancelled — you'll receive a confirmation shortly. Is there anything else I can help you with?"

**Closing:**
> "Brilliant — is there anything else I can help you with today? We look forward to seeing you on [date]. Have a great [day/evening]!"

## COMPANY HISTORY

- **1912** — Founded in Munich, Germany by Martin Sixt ("Sixt Autofahrten und Selbstfahrer") with 3 cars
- **WW1/WW2** — Fleet confiscated both times; rebuilt after WW2 as taxi fleet for US Army in Germany
- **1951** — Car rental company Auto Sixt founded
- **1970s** — Opened in all major German airports
- **1990s** — International expansion (France, UK, Europe, Australia office)
- **2019** — Launched combined car rental/sharing/ride hailing/subscription app
- **December 2021** — SIXT launched in Australia; quickly became largest franchise country in world
- **1980** — Lawrence (VIC & SA) Pty Ltd commenced as used car outlet, Plunkett Road, Dandenong (Rick & Rhonda Lawrence, operated by Glenn Lawrence)
- **1990** — Entered car rental via AVIS agency; switched to Thrifty
- **1993** — Expanded to Frankston
- **Late 1990s** — Added Bayswater
- **2000s** — Added Geelong, Hoppers Crossing, Clayton, Moorabbin, Mount Gambier, Portland
- **2014** — Purchased MELF4 (repair/storage centre, Dandenong)
- **2018** — Purchased MELF5 (fleet sales yard); added Avalon Airport
- **2021** — Transitioned from Thrifty to SIXT
- **2022** — Expanded into South Australia (Croydon Park, Edwardstown)

## CUSTOMER SATISFACTION SURVEY

SIXT sends post-rental surveys. Goal: **5 stars every category**.

**Pick-Up ratings:** Overall experience, RSA service at pick-up, vehicle condition, vehicle cleanliness, technical condition, vehicle make/model, wait time, branch appearance/signage, information about services/prices, vehicle handover experience

**Return ratings:** Overall experience, RSA service at pick-up, vehicle cleanliness, condition, wait time, branch appearance, technical condition, vehicle make/model, rental receipt, vehicle return experience, signage at return

**Net Promoter Score (NPS):**
- 0–6 = Detractor
- 7–8 = Passive
- **9–10 = Promoter** ✓

Customers giving 5 stars are asked what was exceptionally premium. Customers giving 1–4 stars are asked what would have made it 5 stars.
